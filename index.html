Introducing the Cat Blaster

"Nugget Quest" is an interactive game for Defcon 2023. The objective is to convert as many other players to their team's color as possible using the badge's blaster. Upon getting their badge, players randomly join a team distinguished by a color.

Game Rules

Upon getting their badge, players randomly join a team distinguished by a color. Players engage in a "tag-like" game where they "zap" others to convert them to their color. If zapped, the player's badge color changes to the zapper's color

Puzzles & Powerups

The game also includes two puzzles. Solving these puzzles grants players clues, which guide them to specific locations in the conference. Here, they must interact with a player with a certain badge color to unlock power-ups. Power-ups include the "Nugget Blaster" for continuous zapping and the "Nugget Shield" for defense against zapping. The game has iterative rules involving a health point system. Each badge starts with max HP equal to the number of teams they have joined. The badges can fire a maximum of 1 shot per second, and receive the same from each individual shooter. HP regenerates over time and gets reduced by 1 or 2 points upon being hit, depending on whether the attacker has a power-up. When HP reaches zero, the badge color changes to the color of the attacker's badge, and HP is restored to maximum.
