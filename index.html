Introducing the Cat Blaster

"Nugget Quest" is an interactive game for Defcon 2023. The objective is to convert as many other players to their team's color as possible using the badge's blaster. Upon getting their badge, players randomly join a team distinguished by a color.

Game Rules

Upon getting their badge, players randomly join a team by a color: red, green, blue, purple, and white. Players engage in a "tag-like" game where they "zap" others to convert them to their color. If zapped, the player's badge color changes to the zapper's color

Puzzles & Powerups

Puzzles
The game also includes two puzzles. Solving these puzzles grants players clues, which guide them to specific locations in the conference. Here, they must interact with a player with a certain badge color to unlock power-ups.

Nugget Blaster
The Nugget Blaster powerup unlocks continuous zapping to shoot ammo without delay.

Nugget Shield
The Nugget Shield powerup provides defense against zapping.

Health
The badges can fire a maximum of 1 shot per second, and receive the same from each individual shooter. HP regenerates over time and gets reduced by 1 or 2 points upon being hit, depending on whether the attacker has a power-up.

About the Nugget

The Nugget is a cat-shaped hacking console used to teach the basics of ethical hacking. The Nugget is what powers the game using the ESP32 microcontroller.
